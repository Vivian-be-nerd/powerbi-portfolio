## Real-world Data Analysis

### [LA Crime Analysis (2020-2024)](./project1-la-crime/)

**Project Overview**  
Geospatial analysis of 375,000+ LAPD crime records identifying crime patterns, hotspots, and temporal trends to inform public safety strategies.

**Key Highlights**
- 375K+ Part 1 crime records from LAPD public database
- Interactive crime hotspot mapping with geospatial visualization
- Temporal pattern analysis showing peak crime hours (noon & 6 PM)
- 70% property crime concentration in commercial areas
- Actionable policy recommendations for patrol optimization

**Technologies:** Power BI Desktop, Power Query (ETL), DAX (SWITCH, CALCULATE), Spatial-Temporal Analysis

### [Employee Attrition Analysis](./project2-hr-attrition/)

**Project Overview**  
Comprehensive HR analytics examining 40-year workforce trends to identify attrition drivers and develop retention strategies.

**Key Highlights**
- 1,470 employee records spanning 40 years
- 133% increase in attrition rate from 2015 to 2024
- Identified burnout as primary driver beyond compensation
- Retention strategy recommendations for HR stabilization

### [Used Car Sales Performance](./project3-car-sales/)

**Project Overview**  
Regional sales analysis optimizing pricing strategies and inventory management for automotive retail.

**Key Highlights**
- 10 years of sales data across 18 states and 27 agents
- 3,700% sales growth with 47% CAGR (2015-2024)
- Regional car-type preferences driven by urbanization patterns
- Critical pricing inefficiency identified ($47 range across all mileage segments)

**Technologies:** Power BI Desktop (5-page dashboard), Power Query, DAX, Geospatial analysis, CAGR calculations

### [Banking Customer Retention](./project4-banking/)

**Project Overview**  
Churn pattern analysis identifying behavioral indicators to develop targeted customer retention strategies.

**Key Highlights**
- U-shaped churn pattern (both low-usage and high-usage segments at 20%+ churn)
- Premium cardholders showing unexpectedly higher churn than basic users
- Systematic support failure: 56% of churned customers contacted bank 2-4 times without resolution
- Proactive engagement strategies recommended

**Technologies:** Power BI Desktop, Power Query, DAX (utilization rates, churn indicators), Customer segmentation analysis
